# SRJ International — Keyword & Topic Map

_Date: 2026-07-15 · Method: SEO-Resources keyword-clustering + topical-maps guides · Grounded in `ideal-client-profile.md` and `gate-2-map.md`_

## The one rule this follows
**One search intent = one keyword cluster = one page.** We do not write a page per keyword; we group keywords by the intent behind them and build one strong page per group.

## Who we're targeting (from the ICP)
Founder-led **UK digital/marketing agencies**, £500k–£5m turnover, ~30% margin, scaling toward a **5–10 year exit**. Pains: corporation tax, VAT, **and personal/director tax**; high costs; staying competitive. They find advisers via **social/referral**, then book a call. Decides fast, tech-savvy.

That ICP shapes the keywords: this is a **niche** ("...for marketing agencies" / "...for digital agencies"), not generic "small business accountant". The niche is the whole advantage: we can't outrank Crunch/Xero for "small business accountant", but we can own "accountant for marketing agencies".

## Writing rules (applied to every page, from the guides)
- Answer the core question in the **first 40–60 words** under a question-style H2 (wins featured snippets + AI citations).
- Clusters: **1,200–2,000 words**; pillar: **1,500–2,500+**.
- Primary keyword in **title, H1, URL, first paragraph**; secondaries in H2/H3. No stuffing.
- **2–5 internal links** per post (up to the pillar + 2 sibling clusters).
- Named author **Simon Jacobs (CTA · ACA, ex-PwC)** on every post (E-E-A-T — already wired into schema).
- FAQ block + `FAQPage` schema on the pillar and any post with a clear Q&A.

## Proof gap (parallel track, not content but ranking-critical)
